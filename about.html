JOHN KYLE COOPER

Hi, thanks for visiting my site.

I recently obtained my Master's degree in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Rochester with a research focus in auditory neuroscience. During my masters I independently conducted a research study of the "effects of audio-visual congruence on the auditory brainstem response to speech". I have a passion for understanding human hearing and the use of technology to aid those who experience deafness or hearing loss. I have past experience in research on the study of the inner ear and the cochlea through the use of Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT).
