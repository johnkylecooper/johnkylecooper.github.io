JOHN KYLE COOPER

Hi, thanks for visiting my site.

I have a passion for understanding human hearing and the use of technology to aid those who experience deafness or hearing loss.

I have been awarded a Belgian American Educational Foundation (BAEF) fellowship to study under Dr. Tom Francart and the Experimental OtoRhinoLaryngology (ExpORL) laboratory at KU Leuven in Beglium.

I recently obtained my Master's degree in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Rochester with a research focus in auditory neuroscience. During my masters I independently conducted a research study on the "effects of audio-visual congruence on the auditory brainstem response to speech".

I have past experience in research on the study of the inner ear and the cochlea through the use of Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT). This research was conducted at Texas A&M University.
